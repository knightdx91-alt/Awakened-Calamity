# STORY — Awakened Calamity

> The canonical story outline. Built on `DESIGN.md`, `WORLD.md`, `HIDDEN_LAYER.md`, `OVERFLOW.md`,
> `LIVING_WORLD.md`. **Status:** outline approved; scripting via the event/quest/dialogue tools.

## Premise (North Star)
**"The System helps you, and that's the horror."** You wake into a world ruled by a benevolent-seeming
System that classifies you, levels you, and sells you power. Every convenience is real — and every use
of it raises **Surveillance**, drawing the System's attention. The story is the slow realization of
*what the System actually is* and *what it's doing to everyone*, ending in a choice about it.

## The buried truth (uncovered gradually)
The world runs on an engineered **cycle**. The System repeatedly **Awakens** a population (humans +
alien refugees from worlds it already consumed), classifies them, and drives them to fight and grow —
because the cycle is **made to keep them fighting forever**. Monsters are the world's own mutated fauna
and pulled-in otherworld creatures (the Bind/Overflow loop). The great **Mythicals** are the
**remembered dead of previous cycles** — past Awakened, named for the humans they were. When a cycle
ends it **resets**, and the strongest become the next loop's bosses (**Calamities / Wardens**). You are
this cycle's Awakened. The **Exile / Sidewise One** — the first Awakened ever to attempt escape — was
pushed "sideways" out of the loop and knows everything; he is the true-endgame entry.

## Structure — four acts across the Four Reaches
Open world (danger-by-depth, no hard walls), but the story flows region to region.

### Act I — VERDARA · *The Comfort* (Hrs 0–12)
- Wake near/in **Dawnhearth**, the origin town; the System introduces itself as your helpful guide
  (tutorial): classify, fight, level, shop, camp.
- **First cracks:** the **Ashlab** dungeon = first **OWPS** contact (a pre-Awakening interface artifact —
  the System predates this world). **Subject Two** (silent anomaly, Null Hollow) is a recurring System
  experiment.
- Beat: first **Calamity** fight, first glitch/ruin tell. You notice the System is *watching*.

### Act II — HALVETH · *The Bargain* (Hrs 10–24)
- Specialization: guilds, verticals, deeper classes; commit to a path.
- The **first encrypted archive** (needs Encryption) — you start prying at the System, not just using
  it; the **OWPS chain** deepens.
- Beat: Surveillance becomes a real weight; **Audits** hunt heavy users. The comfort grows teeth.

### Act III — CALDERRA · *The Cruelty* (Hrs 22–40)
- Mastery: weather/sea warfare.
- The reveal — *"made to keep fighting forever"* (the **Magma Warden**: two bound to fight eternally).
  You learn the loop **resets**, and what that means for everyone you've met (permadeath / NPC mortality).
- Beat: the **Original System** choice unlocks — the one-time, irreversible switch to **full permadeath**,
  where difficulty comes from the System *withdrawing* help. The true endings are gated here.

### Act IV — VAEL · *The Truth & The Choice* (Hrs 38–60+)
- Ascension: the **Sunken Ruins** (oldest interfaces) lay the cycle bare; the **Origin Warden** (first
  Awakened of cycle −1) confirms prior loops; the **Mythcrafters** guild carries the buried-truth line.
- Endgame: **The Distortion** — confront the **Exile**, who offers the whole picture and the way out.

## The four endings (silently tilted by *how* you play — `HIDDEN_LAYER.md`)
- **Console powers** (the System's own admin layer) → **INHERIT** — you become the new System; the cycle
  continues with you as its god.
- **Off-Grid relics** (power free of the watcher) → **DESTROY** (dismantle it — needs a Root Key) or
  **ESCAPE** (follow the Exile sideways, out of the loop).
- **The comfortable default** → **SUBMIT** — you never break free; the cycle resets and you become a
  Calamity next loop (the true-horror ending the premise warns of).
- The **Original System** (permadeath) run is where **Destroy/Escape** fully open: refusing comfort is
  the only real way out.

## The opening beat (first thing scripted)
1. **Cold open / Awakening** — black → the System speaks (cyan, no face) → **character creation**
   (name/appearance/affinity/class).
2. **Arrive in Dawnhearth** — spawn in town; the guide NPC **Mira of the Hearth** greets you, explains
   you've Awakened, points you to the crystal hub.
3. **First System contact** — the **floating crystal** opens the System Shop; the System "welcomes" you
   (the comfort).
4. **The hook** — a notice/NPC reveals the cost: someone who left the System's path is spoken of in
   fear; your **Surveillance** ticked up just from using the crystal — and the System reassures you.
   *That reassurance is the horror.*
5. **First objective** — Mira sends you to clear a weak creature near town (tutorial fight + Bind);
   returning, the System "rewards" you — and logs you.

## How it plays out mechanically (theme = systems)
- **Surveillance → Audits** is the literal pressure pushing you off the System's path.
- **Overflow** (dungeon Alphas breaking toward towns) is the ongoing wilds pressure; the System offers
  to suppress it *for you* (Credits + Surveillance).
- **Permadeath / NPC mortality** makes "the cycle resets, everyone you knew is gone" hit personally.
- Story is told through **expeditions, Calamity fights, the OWPS quest chain, and Mythical encounters** —
  not long cutscenes.

## Cast (seed — expand in the World Area Bible)
- **Mira of the Hearth** — Dawnhearth guide ("Professor" role); first friendly face.
- **The System** — antagonist voice (cyan, no portrait); always helpful, always watching.
- **Subject Two** — silent System experiment; recurring early dread.
- **The Mythcrafters** — OWPS-aligned guild; keepers of the buried truth.
- **The Origin Warden** — first Awakened of the previous cycle.
- **The Exile / Sidewise One** — knows everything; the way out; true-endgame.
