Affinity badge — the 9 affinities (Ember, Tide, Verdant, Storm, Stone, Frost, Toxin, Umbral, Lumen) and 2 meta-types (Corruption, Untethered, both dashed). Solid pill keyed to the affinity color.

```jsx
<AffinityBadge affinity="ember" />
<AffinityBadge affinity="umbral" />
<AffinityBadge affinity="corruption" />
```

Meta-types render with a dashed border to signal their System/resistance status.
